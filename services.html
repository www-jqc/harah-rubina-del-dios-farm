Our Services

Discover the various experiences we offer at Harah Rubina Del Dios Farm

Coffee Shop

Experience our cozy coffee shop with a rural charm

Take a break and enjoy our freshly brewed coffee while taking in the serene farm atmosphere. Our coffee shop offers:

  • Local and imported coffee selections
  • Fresh pastries and snacks
  • Comfortable seating area
  • Perfect spot for casual meetings

Entertainment Zone

Your destination for fun and entertainment

Enjoy quality time with friends and family in our entertainment area featuring:

  • Modern karaoke rooms
  • Professional billiards tables
  • Comfortable seating arrangements
  • Food and beverage service available

Farm Tours

Experience authentic farm life and learn about agriculture

Join our educational farm tours and discover:

  • Guided tours of our farm
  • Learn about local agriculture
  • Photo opportunities
  • Interactive farming experiences

Biker's Stop

A perfect rest stop for cycling enthusiasts

We welcome bikers with facilities designed for their comfort:

  • Bike parking area
  • Refreshment station
  • Rest area with scenic views
  • Basic bike maintenance tools

About Us

Harah Rubina Del Dios Farm, owned by Luisita Oponda, is a premier destination in Santo Niño Purok II, Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, offering diverse recreational activities and authentic farm experiences.
